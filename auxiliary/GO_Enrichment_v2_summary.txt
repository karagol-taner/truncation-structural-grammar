METHODS BLOCK (paste into Methods)
----------------------------------------
Functional enrichment was performed with g:Profiler (gprofiler-official Python client) against a custom background comprising the unique gene symbols of all canonical proteins that entered the AFDB-multimer pipeline (n=4114). Gene Ontology (Biological Process, Molecular Function, Cellular Component), Reactome and KEGG annotations were queried simultaneously. Multiple-testing correction used g:SCS at alpha=0.05; Benjamini-Hochberg FDR is reported as a second column. Terms were filtered to annotation size 5 <= |T| <= 1000 and intersection size >= 5 to exclude uninformative super-terms and noise. A robustness re-analysis was performed on the strict gain-of-function subset, restricting both query and background to canonicals with ipTM < 0.5; only terms significant in BOTH analyses are reported as 'robust'.
